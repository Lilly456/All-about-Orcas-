Orcas different names and meanings

  • Killer Whale:This was a very common name for these animals. The name comes from an interesting story. “Overseas fisherman saw the Orcas hunting large whales. The fisherman told people that the orcas were whale killers but someone messed it up and got it backwards so instead of whale-killers they became killer whales.”

  • Orcas:The name Orca comes from the name Orcinus Orcas which is translated as ‘demon of the underworld’. Another translation is, ‘of or belonging to the kingdom of the dead.
