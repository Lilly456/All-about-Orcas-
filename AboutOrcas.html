A little bit about Orcas!

An orca is a marine mammal. They are often confused for being a whale because of their name ‘killer whale’, but did you know that orcas are actually dolphins. Orcas are the largest member of the dolphin family.Orca’s can be found in many oceans around the world. They are incredibly intelligent animals, and are highly adaptable when it comes to where they live or hunt. Orcas are one of the fastest swimmers, which helps them when they are hunting for food. “Orcas have been recorded at amazing speeds of up to 54kph!
